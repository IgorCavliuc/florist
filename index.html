Turnkey interior phytodesign

I create a professional phytodesign for your interior. To begin with, together with the client, I develop a design project, taking into account all his wishes and tastes. Then I choose the plants that are best suited to bring the project to life. Finally, the job is done and you can enjoy your renovated interior. All work takes no more than 2-3 weeks!
